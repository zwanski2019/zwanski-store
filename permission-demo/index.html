What Just Happened?

This demonstration simulated how modern web applications can request and potentially abuse browser permissions. In a real-world attack scenario, malicious actors could use these techniques to:

  • Track your precise location in real-time
  • Access your camera and microphone without your knowledge
  • Read and modify your clipboard contents
  • Monitor your device orientation and motion
  • Request notification permissions to deliver malicious payloads

How to Protect Yourself

Review Permissions

Regularly audit and manage website permissions in your browser settings.

Use Privacy Extensions

Consider using uBlock Origin, Privacy Badger, or similar tools to block unwanted tracking.

HTTPS Everywhere

Ensure you only use websites with valid SSL certificates.

Keep Software Updated

Regularly update your browser and operating system to patch known vulnerabilities.
