About Zwanski Tech

Founder: Mohamed Ibrahim Zwanski

Zwanski Tech is a resilient, founder-led IT solutions platform designed to empower individuals and small businesses through innovative digital tools and education. Founded and meticulously built from the ground up by Mohamed Ibrahim Zwanski—a self-taught tech visionary whose story embodies transformation amid adversity—the company reflects a deep commitment to accessibility, security, and community-driven growth.

Mohamed's journey began in the midst of political instability in Niger, where survival meant turning discarded electronics into makeshift learning tools. What started as a personal quest for knowledge evolved into a passion for coding, cybersecurity, and system administration. Today, as the sole developer and operator, Mohamed has handcrafted every aspect of Zwanski Tech, including the website at zwanski.bio and zwanski.org, using modern technologies to create a space for practical resources and support. His expertise spans web development, UI/UX design, and IT troubleshooting, driven by the mantra "Why not me?"—a reminder that persistence can bridge any gap.

Mission

Our mission is to deliver secure, accessible, and innovative technology solutions for all, with a focus on ethical innovation, data privacy, and empowerment.
